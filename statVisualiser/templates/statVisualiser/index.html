Stats & Stuff

By Chris and Luke

Welcome to stats n stuff, a webapp which allows you to mess around with some basic statistical models:

Distributions

Allows for rapid visualisation of some graphs, some basic computations surrounding each model, allows for examination of the convolution of two random variables.

The Law of Large Numbers

A demonstration of applying the law of large numbers to the binomial distribution when it is valid. This concerns both the normal and poisson approximations

Generate Dataset

Lets you quickly create your own dataset based on the statistical distributions, see how it compares to its distribution. You can download the data for other uses.
